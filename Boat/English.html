About

We provide the following professional services to assist developers in constructing projects in the most cost-effective manner.

Outsourcing management of onshore/offshore assembly

We provide outsourcing management of the wind turbine tower assembly works, onshore and offshore. Globally certified resources shall be provided to meet project specifications.

Anti-scouring protection installation

DFCC is a proven sub-contractor for anti-scouring protection engineering for offshore wind turbine generators. We provide cost-effective scour protection services locally for any international installation teams.

Crew Transfer and Logistics support

DFCC can arrange and support crew transfer and materials shipment logistics during and after installation project.

Shipping Agency

We provide best quality of shipping agency service for offshore wind farm fleet.

Divers Management Service

Depending on each project's requirements, We serves to recruit and manage qualified divers to carry out designated subsea tasks.

About Us

Founded on 1912, DFCC Marine Engineering inherited 112 years of local industry experience and knowledge. As a seasoned local player with continuous investment on new technologies, we are proud to provide the most productive and high quality engineering services to support customer projects, including the booming offshore wind farm development.
